About Me

Hi! I'm Rakesh, a passionate researcher with expertise in computer vision, AI, and data science. I specialize in developing vision-based applications, from real-time image and video analysis to deep learning models for detection and segmentation. I enjoy building practical tools with real-world impact and am currently focusing on 3D vision, exploring areas such as robotic navigation, 3D mapping (SLAM), Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF), and Gaussian Splatting, to create high-quality solutions for real-life applications.
